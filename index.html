About Us

The KSRCT Institution’s Innovation Council takes pride in fostering innovation and entrepreneurship within its institute housed within a dedicated space, the Institution’s Innovation Council provides a platform for students and faculty to collaborate, ideate, and bring their innovative ideas to fruition. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and resources, the council supports various initiatives aimed at promoting creativity and problem-solving skills among its members. We organize workshops, seminars, and hackathons to nurture the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship among its members. Additionally, the council facilitates industry collaborations and partnerships to bridge the gap between academia and the corporate world.

"Creativity is thinking up new things. Innovation is doing new things”
