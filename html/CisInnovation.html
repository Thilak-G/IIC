National Innovation and Startup Policy

The NISP 2019 for students and faculty of HEIs will enable the institutes to actively engage students, faculties and staff in innovation and entrepreneurship related activities. This framework will also facilitate MHRD in bringing uniformity across HEIs in terms of Intellectual Property ownership management, technology licensing and institutional Startup policy, thus enabling creation of a robust innovation and Start up ecosystem across all HEIs

Tamil Nadu Startup and Innovation Policy

The Tamil Nadu Startup and Innovation Policy 2018-2023, aims to provide an enabling, innovative ecosystem in the State. Implementation of the policy will enable the State to emerge as the “Knowledge Capital” and “Innovation Hub” of the country. It will also attract entrepreneurs and investors across the globe.
