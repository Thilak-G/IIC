Career Guidance and Corporate Relationship (CGCR)


Vision


To enhance internships based on Career and Professional Development so as to empower students to discover and pursue a path to a fulfilling career


Mission


To explore and pursue meaningful career development, and simultaneously inspiring Institution to consistently reinforce career prospects by internships

Roles and Responsibilities

  • To facilitate the student with the development of an internship contract that will be negotiated with the field instructors
  • To demonstrate an interest in and commitment to student academics with a willingness to assign staff time and energy to intern supervision.
  • To provide adequate training and supervision to make internships a real learning experience
  • To provide the tools, materials and equipment needed for the intern for the able completion of cost productive assignments.
  • To enable students to delegate the responsibilities requiring the use of administrative skills, under the guidance and leadership of their supervising administrators.
  • To establish a supervisory structure for students’ progress monitoring and feedback process to ensure the internships are proceeding as planned.
