Incubation Cell(IC)

Vision


The Incubation Cell strives to foster entrepreneurship and innovation by providing support in the form of mentorship, training, and resources to transform ideas into viable businesses. It envisions a dynamic and innovative environment that encourages and supports the entrepreneurial endeavours of faculty, staff, and students, leading to economic growth and societal impact.


Mission


Our mission is to catalyse the growth of startups by providing a conducive ecosystem for ideation, incubation, and acceleration. Through strategic partnerships and collaborations, we aim to empower aspiring entrepreneurs to develop innovative solutions that address real-world challenges, driving economic prosperity and fostering a culture of innovation.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • To provide increased opportunities for student engagement and one-on-one learning
  • To organise hackathons, codeathons, idea competitions, and mini-challenges in collaboration with industries.
  • To promote entrepreneurship and innovation among students and faculty members.
  • To facilitate the protection of innovations through Intellectual Property Rights and guide incubates through the procedures
  • To formalate involved in securing patents, copyrights, trademarks, and trade secrets.
  • To ensure the timely execution of innovation and entrepreneurship-related activities as prescribed by the Central Ministry of Innovation and Entrepreneurship Cell (MIC).
