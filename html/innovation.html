Innovation, Intellectual Property Rights - Research & Development (IC)

Vision


Research and Innovation team strives to make discoveries, publications, mentoring and training, and in the translation of research into health and economic benefits. Research and Innovation envisions a dynamic, innovative, and diversified environment that will promote and support the research and creative scholarly activities of faculty, staff, and students.


Mission


To collaboratively transform the delivery of health care through implementation and evaluation of researches, and thereby promoting innovative approaches to high quality, safe care, improved patient outcomes at lower costs.

Roles and Responsibilties

  • To organize hackathons, codeathons, idea competition, mini-challenges, etc., with the collaboration of industries.
  • To foster and facilitate literary works, artistic works, scientific works, broadcasts, phonograms, scientific discoveries, inventions in varied fields of human endeavour, trademarks, service marks, industrial designs, patents, copyrights, trademarks, service marks and trade secrets.
  • To protect innovations by means of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) adhering to the procedures and formalities framed by the team.
  • To conduct an innovation and entrepreneurship-related activities prescribed by Central MIC in time bound fashion
  • To Identify and reward innovations and share success stories
  • To organize periodic workshops/ seminars/ interactions with entrepreneurs, investors, professionals and create a mentor pool for student innovators
